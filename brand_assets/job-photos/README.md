# Job Photos — drop before/after hauls here

Drop your job photos into this folder. Because the project syncs through Google Drive, anything you
put here shows up for Claude automatically — no need to "send" each one. Then just say
**"add the new job photos to the site"** and Claude builds them into the home + service-area pages.

## How to get photos here
- **From your Mac:** drag the image files straight into this folder.
- **From your phone:** AirDrop to the Mac → drop them in here, OR add them to a Google Drive folder
  that syncs to this location.

## Naming (so Claude can pair + place them correctly)
Use: `town-jobtype-before.jpg` and `town-jobtype-after.jpg`

Examples:
- `paso-robles-garage-before.jpg` / `paso-robles-garage-after.jpg`
- `slo-mattress-after.jpg`
- `atascadero-yardwaste-before.jpg`

Towns: paso-robles · slo · atascadero · arroyo-grande (or `general` if it's not town-specific).

## Quality / privacy checklist (see SEO PDF §5C)
- Landscape, good light; matched angle for before/after.
- Keep phone GPS on (geotag helps local SEO).
- No faces, house numbers, license plates, mail, or personal documents.

Claude will optimize/resize them for the web and place them in galleries on the relevant pages.
