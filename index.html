FAQ

Junk Removal in San Luis Obispo County — Your Questions, Answered

How much does junk removal cost in San Luis Obispo County?

Pricing depends on the volume and type of items being hauled. Text us some photos or give us a call and we will give you a clear, upfront price before we lift a single item — no hidden fees or surprises. Reach us at (805) 424-5727 or fill out our online form.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of San Luis Obispo County, including San Luis Obispo, Paso Robles, Atascadero, Arroyo Grande, Pismo Beach, Morro Bay, Nipomo, Templeton, Los Osos, and Grover Beach.

Do you offer same-day junk removal?

Yes — we offer same-day and next-day pickups whenever our schedule allows. Call us early at (805) 424-5727 to check availability for your area.

What items can you haul away?

We haul furniture, mattresses, appliances, electronics, construction debris, yard waste, and full home, office, estate, and storage-unit cleanouts. If you're not sure about an item, just ask.

Do I need to be home for the pickup?

Not always. As long as we can safely access the items and you've approved the quote, we can often complete the job without you being on site. We'll coordinate the details when you book.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Joy Junk Removal is fully licensed and insured, so your home and property are protected on every job.
