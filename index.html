FAQ

Junk Removal in San Luis Obispo County — Your Questions, Answered

How much does junk removal cost in San Luis Obispo County?

Pricing is based on how much space your items take in the truck. We give you a clear, upfront price before we start — and you only pay for what we actually haul, never a padded max charge. Text a photo or call (805) 424-5727 for a free quote.

Will I be charged the full price even if the truck isn't full?

No. You pay for the space your junk actually takes up — not a flat max fee. The quote is the price, with no surprises.

Will the price go up once you start the job?

No — the price we quote is the price you pay. We never change rates in the middle of a job or tack on hidden fees, and you only pay for the space your junk actually takes in the truck. What we quote is what you pay. 👍

Do you offer same-day junk removal?

Yes — same-day and next-day pickups are often available. Call early at (805) 424-5727 and we'll do our best to get to you today.

What items do you take?

Furniture, mattresses, appliances, electronics, hot tubs, construction debris, yard waste, and full home, garage, office, and estate cleanouts. If you're not sure about an item, just ask.

Do you remove hot tubs?

Yes — we remove and haul away old hot tubs and spas. Send us a photo for a quick quote.

Do you do full house or estate cleanouts?

Yes — from a single item to a whole house, including the heavy, awkward stuff. We leave it broom-clean.

Do you do apartment & student move-out cleanouts?

Yes — move-outs are one of our busiest jobs. Whether you're a Cal Poly student clearing out a dorm or apartment or a family moving across town, we haul the old couch, mattress, and leftover junk fast — often same-day — so you get your deposit back. 🎓

Do you confirm what you're taking before you haul it?

Always. We walk through everything with you and only take what you point out — nothing leaves without your okay.

What areas do you serve?

All of San Luis Obispo County — San Luis Obispo, Paso Robles, Atascadero, Arroyo Grande, Pismo Beach, Morro Bay, Nipomo, and nearby towns.

Do I need to be home for the pickup?

Not always. As long as we can safely access the items and you've approved the quote, we can often complete the job without you on site.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Joy Junk Removal is fully licensed and insured, so your home and property are protected on every job.

How do I get a quote?

Text a few photos or call (805) 424-5727, or use the form at joyjunkremoval.com. Free, no obligation.
