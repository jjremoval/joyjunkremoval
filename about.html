Why We Started 💛

Joy Junk Removal was founded with a simple mission: make getting rid of junk as easy and stress-free as possible. Too many people were putting off cleanouts because they didn't know who to call, or were worried about being overcharged and underserved.

We built Joy around the opposite: upfront pricing, friendly crews, and same-day availability whenever possible. Whether you need a single couch hauled away or a full estate cleaned out, we treat every job — and every customer — with the same care.
